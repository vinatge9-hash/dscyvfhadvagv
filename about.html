Our Story

La Marisq started as a small pop-up by the harbor. Our chefs believed that the best dishes come from the freshest ingredients and a love for the sea. Over the years we've grown while keeping the same commitment to seasonal, sustainable seafood and warm hospitality.

We partner with local fishermen and farmers to bring daily-changing flavors to your table. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a celebratory dinner, our team crafts every meal with intention.
